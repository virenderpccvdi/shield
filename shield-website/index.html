What Shield does

Everything your family needs, in one app

A complete digital safety platform for modern families — works on any Android device, any network.

Works everywhere

DNS-level content filtering blocks harmful websites across all apps — on WiFi, school networks, and mobile 4G/5G simultaneously.

Smart schedules

Daily time budgets, bedtime lockdowns, and per-app limits. Healthy digital habits built in, adjustable any time.

Live location

Real-time GPS tracking with geofence alerts. Know when your child arrives at school or leaves a safe zone — instantly.

Instant alerts

Family controls

Rewards system
