2 Description of Service

Shield is a family internet protection platform that enables parents and guardians to manage and safeguard their children's online experience. The Service currently includes the following features:

  • DNS Filtering: Blocks access to websites and online content in categories such as adult content, gambling, violence, phishing, and malware, using DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) technology. Filtering applies on any network — home WiFi, school WiFi, and mobile 4G/5G data.
  • Parental Controls: Content category blocking, custom allow/block lists, internet schedule enforcement (e.g. no internet during school hours or after bedtime), and per-device screen time budgets.
  • Location Tracking: Real-time GPS location sharing for enrolled child devices, location history, and geofence alerts that notify parents when a child enters or leaves a designated area.
  • Alerts & Notifications: Push notifications and optional messaging-app alerts (WhatsApp, Telegram) for key events such as blocked site attempts, geofence breaches, and SOS requests from a child.
  • Rewards System: A points-based incentive system allowing parents to assign tasks to children and reward compliance with screen time or other privileges.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Summaries of browsing activity categories, time-online trends, and weekly digest emails.
  • ISP & Multi-Tenant Deployment: White-label deployment for Internet Service Providers and enterprises to offer Shield as a managed service to their customers.

We reserve the right to modify, enhance, or discontinue any feature of the Service at any time with reasonable notice where practicable.
